About Me

Hi, I'm a 14 year-old developer who has been coding for almost 3 years now which basically means I've spent 3 years fighting with bugs, Googling errors at 2AM, and celebrating every time my code actually works without breaking something else. I'm just entered SS3 this new session, so my life is currently a mix of exams, daily learning 😭, playing football with my friends ⚽, assignments, and coding 💻💻 whenever I can sneak in some time. Honestl, sometimes my project looks like magic, works smoothly as an engine with new premium engine oil... other times, they look like I gave my computer a headache.

When I finish secondary school, I plan to study Robotics Engineering, web development is currently my starting point, because if coding on a laptop is fun, imagine coding something that actually moves! I want to build robots that can do useful stuff - like helping people, making life easier, or at the very least, carrying my books to school so I don't have to. My dream is to mix softwares and AI with real-world machines, basically making stuff you see in sci-fi movies (except without the partwhere the robots take overthe world... probably🤖😂). For now, I'm just enjoying the process learning new things every day, and adding "young developer" to my CV b4 I can even drive a car
